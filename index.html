About The Game

Tile Sweet: Triple Match is a casual tile matching puzzle game where players collect three identical tiles to clear the board. Inspired by classic triple match mechanics and enhanced with a delightful candy theme, the game combines strategic thinking with relaxing gameplay.

Whether you are looking for a quick break or a long puzzle adventure, Tile Sweet offers hundreds of carefully designed levels that gradually increase in difficulty while introducing exciting mechanics and obstacles.
