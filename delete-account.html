Account & Data Deletion Request

Last Updated: June 28, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your account and associated data for Tile Sweet: Triple Match, developed by Hudigame Studio.

How to Request Deletion

To request deletion of your account and data, send an email to the address below from the email associated with your request:

Email: hdart98@gmail.com
Subject: Delete My Account – Tile Sweet: Triple Match

Please include the following so we can locate and verify your data:

  • Your Google Play Games nickname.
  • Your Player ID (if known).
  • The device and approximate date you last played.

We will verify your request before processing it to protect your account against unauthorized deletion.

What Data Is Deleted

Upon a verified request, we will delete or anonymize the personal data associated with your account, including:

  • Cloud save and gameplay progress (levels, stars, coins, boosters).
  • Leaderboard entries (nickname, scores, ranking).
  • Achievement and reward claim status.
  • Player identifiers linked to your account.

What Data May Be Retained

Some information may be retained where required for legal, security, fraud-prevention or legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Records required to comply with applicable laws or tax obligations.
  • Aggregated or anonymized analytics that can no longer identify you.
  • Information necessary to prevent fraud or abuse.

Processing Time

We aim to acknowledge your request within a few business days and to complete deletion within 30 days, unless a longer period is required by law. We will notify you by email once your request has been processed.

Related Information

For more details on how we handle your information, please see our Privacy Policy. If you have questions, visit our Support page.
